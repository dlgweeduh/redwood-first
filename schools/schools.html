Character. Community. Leadership.

A complete education will no longer be complete without exposing students to specific character building curriculum

The Big Idea

PURPOSE

Use signature strengths & personal values to drive your life with Purpose

AWARENESS

Make strong, conscious choices. Stay in charge of emotions. Use the present moment as a tool.

ENGAGE

Deliberately cultivate leadership, teamwork & success through action.

Drive

Students internally driven to practice resiliency, courage & compassion

A Day in the life of a Redwooder

  • 9:00

    Levels of Listening

    Learn to drive a conversation with curiosity as opposed to just waiting for your chance to speak

  • 9:30

    The Happiness Definition

    The team builds a broader definition of happiness by calling out the myths that trap so many of us.

  • 10:15

    Identify Your Values

    Working in pairs and sharing a Peak Experience, each Member designs a Personal Code.

  • 11:00

    The Saboteur

    Identifying the Saboteur – the internal voice determined to maintain the status quo – including their style, modus operandi and killer phrases - lets us know what to look for so we can stay in charge.

  • 11:15

    Passions & Pursuits

    Everyone’s favourite activity finds all of your interests going head to head in your own World Cup.

  • 1:15

    Brain Tour

    Take a crazy trip in to the working of your brian - see the chemistry in play and learn the tricks to rewire the hardware to your advantage.

  • 1:45

    Landmines

    ‎For every value there is a void - knowing them is invaluable. In epic style Yoda & Vader help us battle it out.

  • 2:15

    Relationship Commitments

    The relationships in your life are essential to building fulfillment long term. What do yours look like? The phrase Selfish Generosty is introduced & explored

  • 2:45

    The Team Pledge

    The culmination of the day is a proud and literal journey as you move forward together, a team of indiviuals with a common objective - support, progress and commitment.

    The prerequisites of making excellent choices are awarness and having a clear and thrilling destination to drive towards. By setting a course, Members will learn to gauge their decisions against a simple standard: does this choice move me closer to the person I want to be?

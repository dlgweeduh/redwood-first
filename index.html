The Project

Character & Values Forum

Our main product is a highly customized series of workshops designed to address specific needs by building the essential skills most associated with excellent leadership. Unique challenges and inquires build the individual and group simultaneously, which then impact the culture at large. Completed in half day, full day, two day or 3 month engagements the results are proven and the advocates vocal.
Speaking Engagements

Since launching the business Redwood’s team of leaders have amassed first hand insight, explored comprehensive research and deployed an arsenal of tools all with the goal of supporting people live more meaningful, fulfilled, complete lives. Sometimes hilarious, sometimes poignant, always original, one of our leaders is the perfect solution to speak at your next conference, off-site, team meeting or assembly.

1-on-1 Mentoring & Coaching

Working directly with Managing Director Scott Malcolm is an intensive and effective experience. Meeting the individual wherever they are at, Redwood’ unique tools and challenges are deployed to drastically and measurably improve fulfillment, performance and personal relationships. Goals are laid out, challenges made and action taken. Conducted over either 6 or 12 one-hour sessions.

Project Benefits

Awareness

Identifying clear priorities comes from unambiguous personal understanding of what matters, what defines you, what holds you back and what value you best provide. Clear priorities lead to clear decisions and excellent execution.

Connection

Renew your commitment to manage and improve relationships – especially the important or difficult ones. Whether it is the nuanced inter-personal relationships with co-workers or the all-encompassing parent-child team, your Redwood relationship work involves assessment, action and improvement. Teams also become united by championing common goals and building understanding.

Accountability

You will hold yourself to a higher standard by making choices that support the priorities and commitments you have announced. Redwood creates a community of people who will champion your accomplishments and hold you responsibility to the pledges you have made. And because it is you who made them they will by nature be intrinsically motivating.

Resiliency

Not only is a life without setbacks, frustration and failure impossible, we wouldn’t want it anyway. Learning to quickly rebound from setbacks and disappointment by managing the internal tapes that play in your head is a major focus of Redwood.

Fulfillment

Enduring happiness - it’s causes, correlations and myths are shockingly misunderstood, especially considering the implications on our lives. Creating rigorous strategies to build fulfillment by aligning your values and actions at work and at home allow you take ownership of your happiness and open a world of options.

Engagement

Once you have articulated the personal elements that define you, a customize plan to deploy these elements in targeted, tactical ways is created. Engagement with your own emotions, goals, family, team and objectives is a direct result of this Action Plan. And when you occasionally and naturally slip in to pre-Redwood habits, this honest engagement quickly re-connects you to possibilities so that momentum is maintained.

About US

Our Story

Scott Malcolm founded Redwood in 2011 after a career at Procter & Gamble and Blackberry. The competiveness and learning of the corporate world thrilled, but he always had been drawn to start his own business in an area in which he could make a big impact. Scott became very interested in character development and lamented the lack of structured inquires schools an organizations provided in this area. Scott left his job and spent 6 months researching and building a product specifically designed to meet this need.

We now have a great team and growing list of Redwood advocates with exceptional stories about the impact Redwood has had on their lives. As the organization grows the team is excited to continue to share the experience and push more people to Grow Big.
